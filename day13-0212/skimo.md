Skimo, short for ski with mountaineering,is the newest winter Olympic sport and will debut at the 2026 games in Milan-cortain.It combines Alpine skiing with mountaineering and is a race against with time where athletes are required to ascend and descend mountianous terrain using skis and other specilised equipment.

At the Olympics,there will be a relay event and also a sprint competition which lasts just few minutes.Athletes start in steep uphill using climbing skins.There are detachable grippy strips you attach to prevent skis to sliding.The athletes then rapaid transition to boot packing where skis carry on a backpack as they run up an incline as faster as possible.There is then a technical downhill course full of tight turns and obstacles.Because of all the transition,races can be won or lost in seconds depending on how smoothly the athletes can switch between modes.
